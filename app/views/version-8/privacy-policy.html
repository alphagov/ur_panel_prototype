Privacy policy

Your privacy is important to us. When you volunteer to test GOV.UK and how it works we will ask you some questions about who you are and how you use the internet. to share information or data about you.

When you answer our questions, we save your information. We only collect personal information that we need to invite you to participate in the right user research session. If you choose to sign up, we may ask you to provide contact and identity information and other personal information. You can choose whether you want to share information with us. You’ll always be told when we collect this information.

The data controller for this information is the Cabinet Office, based at 70 Whitehall, London SW1A 2AS registered with the Information Commissioner’s Office with registration number Z7414053.

This privacy policy applies to collection and storage of your information for the GOV.UK user research community. It does not apply to personal information gathered during research sessions. If you are selected to participate in research, we will give you further information about how we’ll use any personal information you give us during the research session.

We may ask you to share:

  • your name and contact details
  • information about how you use the internet
  • information about your personal circumstances, such as employment, benefits or housing

We may also track:

  • your IP address and what web browser you use
  • information on how you use the GOV.UK user research community site, using cookies and page tagging to track which webpage you were on when you signed up to the GOV.UK user research community

By accepting this privacy policy, you are consenting to our use and disclosure of your personal information as described in this privacy policy. If you do not want us to keep your personal information, please do not register.

What we will do with your data

The Government Digital Service wants to make websites better by learning from the people who use them. The information or data you share with us will only be used for user research for the Government Digital Service. Your answers will also help us only ask you to participate in user research that’s relevant to you.

We won’t share your information with any other organisations. Your information will never be used for marketing, market research or commercial purposes. We won’t pass your details on to other websites.

We will not keep your data for longer than 3 years.

Keeping your data secure

We have procedures and security features in place to keep your data secure on our servers. However, by agreeing to participate in user research activities you should be aware and accept that:

  • the Internet is not completely secure, therefore we cannot guarantee the security of your data during its transmission
  • you transmit information or data over the internet at your own risk

Where we will store your data

The data we collect from you will be stored within the UK and will not leave the UK. Your data will be processed by staff within the UK who work for us or one of our suppliers.

By submitting your personal data, you agree to this transfer, storing or processing. We will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy.

Holding your data

We will only hold your data while it is being used for user research purposes and for no longer than 3 years. You can ask for your data to be removed at any time.

Deactivating and deleting your account

If you ask us to delete your account and data, we will delete your account and the information we hold about you from our databases as soon as reasonably possible.

In some circumstances, we’ll keep information you’ve asked us to remove. This might be to resolve disputes, troubleshoot problems and apply our terms of participation. Due to technical and legal constraints, information is never completely removed from our databases, including stored back up systems. You therefore shouldn’t expect that all of your personally identifiable information will be completely removed from our databases.

You can take yourself off our list at any time.

Disclosing your information

Your information will be stored securely and we will only use it for user research.

We may share your data with other government departments who will use it for user research.

We may pass on your personal information if we have a legal obligation to do so, or if we have to enforce or apply our terms of use and other agreements. This includes exchanging information with other government departments for legal reasons.

Your rights

You can find out what information we hold about you, and ask us not to use any of the information we collect. You can contact [email] to find out what information we hold about you.

You can manage the emails and research participation offers you receive on [webpage].

Links to other websites

This privacy policy only applies to the online forms and surveys you complete when you volunteer to help us research and test GOV.UK. This privacy policy doesn’t cover research you participate in, other content on GOV.UK, or other government services and transactions.

Following a link to another website

If you choose to go to another website from this one, you should read the privacy policy on that website to find out what that site will do with your information.

Notice

We may occasionally change this privacy policy so that it accurately reflects regulation and how we collect and disclose data. All amended terms will automatically be effective 30 days after they are posted on this web page.
